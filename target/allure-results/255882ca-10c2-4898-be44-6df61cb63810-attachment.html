PUT to https://jsonplaceholder.typicode.com/posts/1

Body

    {
    "title": "foo",
    "body": "bar",
    "userId": 1,
    "id": 1
}

Headers

Accept: application/json, application/javascript, text/javascript, text/json
Content-Type: application/json; charset=UTF-8

Curl

curl -v -X PUT 'https://jsonplaceholder.typicode.com/posts/1' -H 'Accept: application/json, application/javascript, text/javascript, text/json' -H 'Content-Type: application/json; charset=UTF-8' -d '{ "title": "foo", "body": "bar", "userId": 1, "id": 1 }'
